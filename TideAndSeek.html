Tide and Seek

Unity Engine Solo Project

FULL GAMEPLAY VIDEO

Made as part of my Games Prototyping Module in my second year at University of Staffordshire, Tide and Seek is a stealth game about a cuttlefish trying to save his Dad from a gang of sharks.

I used the C# Language within Unity to create the mechanics, including using scriptable objects as part of my cutscene system.

Inspired by Games such as Another Crabs Treasure and Hotline Miami. Tide and Seek was developed over the course of 5 week long sprints, it required lots of fast iterations within that time.
