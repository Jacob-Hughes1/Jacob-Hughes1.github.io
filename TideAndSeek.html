Tide and Seek

Unity Engine - Solo Project

Overview

Tide and Seek is a Top-Down Stealth game where you play as a cuttlefish with the ability to camoflauge.
Your dad has been stolen, navigate through handcrafted levels avoiding sharks to get him back.
Made as part of my second year at the University of Staffordshire, this project was created within the Unity Engine, using C# to create all mechanics.
This game also features some some simple cutscene sequences, in which I utilised scriptable objects to create.

KEY DESIGN FEATURES

Bite-Sized Levels

Each level is a short section that utilises instant restarts to create fast paced, trial and error style puzzle-like gameplay similar to Hotline Miami.
Level layouts are designed to be quite tight to force the player to have to encounter the enemies.

Fast Enemy Detection Time

Enemies have a short meter that fills whenever the player is within their view, this gives the player a very short amount of time for them to get out of view.
This encourages the player to stay completely out of sight as being detected will certainly result in failure.

High Enemy Density

To ensure that the player has to focus on correct positioning and timing throughout the level, there is a high level of enemy density.
Enemy vision cones often overlap in order to create tight windows for the player to react and move to progress.

Story Cutscenes

Using scriptable objects, I created custom dialogue cutscenes which play between some of the levels.
The story is about a cuttlefish, his dad has been taken by a gang of sharks from his past and the player must save him.
