Shotgun Master

Unity Engine - Solo Project

Overview

Shotgun Master is a 2D Platformer Game with a focus on game feel and juice.
The goal of the game is to get to the end of the level and defeat all enemies within.
Made as part of my second year at the University of Staffordshire, this project was created within the Unity Engine, using C# to create all mechanics.

KEY DESIGN FEATURES

Powerful Movement Feel

The player is intentionally designed to feel heavy, this is achieved through careful tweaking of gravity, movement speed, particle effects and sound effects. The player is unable to be pushed around by enemies, which creates the feeling that they are much more powerful.

Exaggerated Visual Effects

As one of the main focusses of this project was game juice, lots of visual effects were used to exaggerate the impact of players actions.
Effects such as blood splatters, screenshake and shotgun shell ejection, impact particles and more were all used to enhance the satisfaction while playing the game.

Forgiving Controls

To create the feeling of smooth movement and a polished experience, many forgivessness mechanics have been implemented such as:
Coyote time, Jump Buffering, Variable Jump Height.

Platforming Mechanics

Fundamental platforming mechanics such as smooth moving platfroms, semi-solid platforms and spike obstacles provide an environment for the player to have to utilise their movement.
