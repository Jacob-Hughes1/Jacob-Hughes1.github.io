Made as part of a 5-person group for the Games 4 Change Game Jam 2026. This game was made using Unreal Engine for Fortnite over the course of 48 hours.

As part of my 5 person group, we all focused on lots of different elements of development, I mainly focused on mechanics and logic creation while helping out with all other aspects when my help was needed.

This game is a collection of 4 mini-games each inspired by a stage in the WFP Food System: Production, Processing, Distribution and Consumption.
