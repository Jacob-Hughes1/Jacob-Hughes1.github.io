WFP Food System Minigames

Unreal Engine for Fornite - Group Project

My Role: Generalist Designer

Overview

This project is inspired by the work of the World Food Programme.
Made as part of a 5-person group for the Games 4 Change Game Jam 2026. This project was made over the course of 48 hours.
As part of my 5 person group, we all focussed on lots of different elements of development, I mainly focused on mechanics and logic creation while helping out with all other aspects when my help was needed.
This game is a collection of 4 mini-games each inspired by a stage in the WFP Food System: Production, Processing, Distribution and Consumption.
Each section provides a unique mini-game experience where the players aim to gain as much score as possible, at the end of the game the total score is shown and can be compared to the other players in the game.
