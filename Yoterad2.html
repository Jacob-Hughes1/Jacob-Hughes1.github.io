Yoterad 2: It's About Time

Unreal Engine 4 - Group Project

My Role: Designer and Programmer

Overview

Yoterad 2: It's About Time is an First-Person Shooter and a sequel to the first game I ever made during my time at college, Yoterad.
You play as Yoterad, a cyborg cowboy on a quest to save his horse. He must travel across time and different dimensions to reach his goal.
Made as part of a 2 person group for my final project as part of my Games Design course at South Staffordshire College. I worked with an artist to create this project.
My work on this project was all of the systems design, level design, in-engine development, music creation and mechanic creation utilising Unreal Engine blueprints.

KEY DESIGN FEATURES

Classic Shooter Feel

Each weapon in the game has a single ammo count, with no reload mechanic, the player can found ammo pickups in the level to replenish their ammo count.
This style of gameplay accompanies the art style to create a feeling similar to a retro shooter.

Time-Swap Mechanic

As part of the second level, there is a time-swap mechanic inspired by the Titanfall 2 campaign mission "Effect and Cause".
This mechanic allows the player to swap between two levels, the levels are similar but have some key differences that force the player to have to use the mechanic to progress in the level.

Varied Enemy Types

There are a total of 3 types of enemies within the game: Ranged, Melee and Bomb. These are carefully placed around the levels to create engaging gameplay.
Bomb enemies are a special type of enemy that blows itself up for massive damage a set amount of time after first contact with the player. This makes the bomb enemy extremely dangerous and forces the player to be careful with which enemies they prioritise when they encounter one.

2D Platformer Style Boss Fight

For the final level, the game swaps to a 2D platformer perspective. This is because the player is transported back to the world of the first Yoterad game.
The player must fight the version of Yoterad from the first game, which they do by colliding with him. The boss will jump around the level randomly while firing projectiles. The player must avoid his projectile attacks to not die.
